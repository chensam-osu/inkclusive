About Us

As we progress as a society and learn more about what is and is not acceptable, it is imperative that our language be able to adapt to certain expectations set by communities that are traditionally underrepresented and/or oppressed.

The focus of this application is to provide a simple tool for screening documents for racist langauge. The particular focus is not in slurs or expletives but rather words and phrases that have deeply rooted racists pasts, often unknown to modern day users.
